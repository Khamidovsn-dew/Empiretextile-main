WHO WE ARE

Company overview

EMPIRE TEXTILE — A MANUFACTURER OF ECO BAGS AND 100% COTTON TEXTILES

We specialize in producing eco-friendly bags and natural cotton fabrics. Our clients are based across Europe, and we offer full product customization — from shape and finishing to branding and labeling.

Our production facility is equipped with modern machinery: industrial overlocks, reinforced stitch sewing machines, automatic thread trimming, and an in-house printing department. We manufacture over 1 million items annually.

We manage the entire manufacturing process ourselves — from fabric selection to the final product. This allows us to ensure consistent quality and meet deadlines with confidence.

CUSTOM SOLUTIONS
